Flag Mania!

Each cell has a small chance to have a flag underneath it. When a flag is revealed, you get a random amount of money from that cell which is calculated by multiplying the number in the bottom left of the cell with a multiplyer that will appear in the bottom right of the cell when the flag is revealed.
